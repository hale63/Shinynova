Who We Are

Built on clean work, clear communication, and real care.

Shinynova is a professional cleaning company serving busy households, offices, and commercial spaces that need dependable results without the follow-up stress. We plan every visit around your priorities, your schedule, and the standards your space deserves.

Our cleaners are trained to work carefully, communicate clearly, and pay attention to the details customers notice: fresh surfaces, tidy edges, sanitized touchpoints, and rooms that feel ready to use again.

Customer satisfaction guides the way we work. From the first quote to the final walkthrough, we keep the process simple, respectful, and consistent so you can feel confident inviting our team in.
